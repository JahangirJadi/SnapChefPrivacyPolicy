Privacy Policy for SnapChef

Effective Date: February 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to SnapChef. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application to extract recipes from video links.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in via Google, we collect your email address, name, and profile picture to create your SnapChef profile.
  • User Content: We store the video URLs you submit and the resulting recipe data (ingredients, steps, images) in our secure database.
  • Inventory Data: If you use the Pantry feature, we store the list of ingredients currently in your kitchen.
  • Payment Data: We use RevenueCat and Google Play Billing to process subscriptions. We do not store your credit card details on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • AI Processing: We share the video transcription with AI models (e.g., GPT-4o, Gemini) to extract recipe details. This data is processed anonymously where possible.
  • Synchronization: We store your recipes in Supabase so you can access them across multiple devices.
  • Usage Tracking: To enforce "Free" vs "Pro" limits, we track the number of extractions performed per month.

4. Third-Party Services

SnapChef integrates with the following third-party providers:

  • Supabase: For database storage and authentication.
  • RevenueCat: For managing in-app subscriptions.
  • Google/YouTube/Instagram: When you provide a link, we access the public content of that URL to generate your recipe.
  • AI Providers: (OpenAI/Google) To provide the extraction service.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You own your data. You may delete your account and all associated recipes at any time within the app settings. Once deleted, we remove your records from our active Supabase production database within 30 days.

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) for data in transit and Row Level Security (RLS) in Supabase to ensure that only you can access your private recipes and pantry.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."
